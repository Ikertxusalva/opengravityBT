1→TimeSerisMomentumTSMOM: 12-month excess return lookback. LONG if 12m return > 0, SHORT if < 0. Position sizing inverse to volatility (vol targeting 10-12% annualized). Monthly rebalancing. Tested: 58 liquid instruments (equities, currencies, commodities, bonds). Sharpe 1.31, annual alpha 20.7%, max drawdown -33.87%. Adaptable to crypto: BTC, ETH, altcoin index. Use rolling 252-day vol or GARCH. Source: Moskowitz et al. 2012 AQR.

2→VolatilityTargeting: Adjust portfolio leverage = Target Vol / Actual Vol. Weekly rebalancing. Calculate leverage using data through t-2 (no look-ahead). Target vol 8-12% annualized. Simple method: 20-day rolling std deviation. Exit positions if vol spike triggers stop. Max leverage 2.0x. Works as risk management layer on any strategy. Improves Sharpe 10-30%, reduces drawdown 20-40%. Source: QuantPedia, Man Group.

3→AdaptiveMovingAverageBreakout: Kaufman Adaptive MA (KAMA). Slow KAMA(10,5,30) for trend filter, Fast KAMA(10,2,30) for entry. LONG: price > slow KAMA + fast > slow (bullish cross). SHORT: price < slow KAMA + fast < slow. ATR(14)-based stop: SL 1.5x ATR, TP 2-3x ATR. Only enter if trending (ATR > 20-day average, not extreme). Daily-Hourly, BTC/ETH/major alts. Win rate 45-55%, Sharpe 1.0-1.5. Source: Kaufman, recent implementations 2024-2025.

4→CarryStrategyStakingLending: LONG top 3 crypto by staking/lending APY (Aave, Compound, native staking ETH/SOL). SHORT bottom 3 or go cash-only on stablecoins at high rates. Monthly rebalancing on yield changes. Equal-weight allocation 1/3 per position. Captures yield differential, not capital appreciation. 5-15% APY expected. Risk: counterparty failure (lending pool hack). Sharpe: High if assets correlated. Source: Quantpedia FX Carry Trade (adapted), DeFi protocols.

5→VolatilityTermStructureArbitrage: Exploit VIX futures contango/backwardation. When futures curve upward-sloped (contango): sell near-term VIX, buy long-term (capture roll-down). When inverted (backwardation): opposite. In crypto: use options IV term structure (Deribit). Monthly roll. Sharpe 1.5-2.5, target 5-15% annualized. Requires tight execution, low transaction costs critical. Source: QuantPedia, Hedge Fund Journal.

6→CrossSectionalMomentum: Rank all assets (top 500 cryptos) by 3/6/12-month returns. LONG top decile (top 10%), SHORT bottom decile. Equal-weight or vol-weight portfolios. Monthly rebalancing on ranking change. Universe selection critical: min $100M-$1B market cap. Exclude stablecoins. Win rate follows momentum mean reversion but crypto reversals faster than equities. Sharpe 1.0-1.5, max drawdown -30 to -50%. Source: Academia, QuantConnect.

7→MeanReversionOrnsteinUhlenbeck: Model crypto pairs (ETH/BTC, etc.) as OU process. Estimate: mean θ, reversion speed μ, volatility σ. Calculate half-life of mean reversion (rolling 60-day). Spread = log(P_A) - β*log(P_B) from cointegration. Z-score > 2: LONG pair (spread oversold). Z-score < -2: SHORT. Exit at z-score 0. LONG entry: spread < mean - 2σ. SL: spread > 3σ. Half-life reliability: 5-60 days optimal. Sharpe 0.8-1.2. Source: Arbitrage Lab, academic papers.

8→RiskParity: Allocate inversely to volatility. Example 4-asset crypto: BTC (50% vol) 40%, ETH (70% vol) 28%, SOL (100% vol) 20%, Stables 12%. Weights = (1/σ_i) / Σ(1/σ_j). Quarterly rebalancing or when weight deviates >10%. Target: equal risk contribution across assets. Lower returns than cap-weight but stable across cycles. Sharpe 0.8-1.2, max drawdown -20 to -30%. Use Hierarchical Risk Parity for crypto (non-linear correlations). Source: QuantPedia, CF Benchmarks.

9→FamaFrenchFactorModel: Run monthly cross-sectional regressions on crypto universe (100+ assets). Factors: Market (aggregate return), Size (small cap - large cap), Value (high P/E - low P/E), Momentum (winner - loser), Liquidity, Quality. Construct long/short portfolios: long top quintile factor exposures, short bottom quintile. Rebalance monthly. In crypto: Momentum + Liquidity factors strongest (Value weak, Size inverted). Sharpe 1.2-1.8 long/short, 0.8-1.2 long-only. Source: CF Benchmarks, academic research 2024.

10→StatisticalArbitrageMultiPair: Johansen cointegration test on all pairs. Find cointegrated pair universe (p-value < 0.05). Signal: spread z-score. Entry LONG spread when z-score < -2 (pair A underperforming). Exit at z-score 0 (mean reversion). Hedge ratio from cointegration: short β*units_of_B per unit_A. Min spread half-life 5-60 days. Universe: 10-50 pairs optimal (vs single pair, reduces variance). Win rate 55-65%, Sharpe 1.0-1.5, market-neutral. Risk: cointegration breaks (LUNA/UST catastrophic failure). Source: Academia, QuantConnect Cointegration League.

11→TailRiskHedging: Buy OTM put options at -20% strike (hedge against 20% crash). Quarterly rolling. Coverage: 50-90% portfolio notional. Cost: 1-3% annualized (drag). Payoff: +100% to +300% during crashes. Alternative: Put spread (buy 80%, sell 90% strike) to reduce cost. For crypto with limited options liquidity: use perpetual short positions as synthetic hedge. Sharpe negative (cost of insurance), but crisis alpha +1000% possible. Source: Hedge Fund Journal, PIMCO.

12→RegimeAdaptiveMeanReversionMomentumBlend: Use ML classifier (Random Forest or LSTM) on 3 states: Trending (ADX>25), Range-bound (ADX<20), Transition. Trending regime: use momentum (EMA crossover, KAMA). Range-bound: use mean reversion (RSI extremes, Bollinger Bands). Transition: 50/50 blend. Retrain regime detector every 60 periods on features: vol, return autocorrelation, ADX, skewness. SL/TP adjust per regime. Sharpe 1.5-2.0, win rate 50-55%, max drawdown -15 to -25%. Outperforms static strategies on regime breaks. Source: arXiv research 2024-2025.

13→MachineLearningHybridSentimentMomentum: Ensemble model (LightGBM + LSTM). Features: Technical (SMA, EMA, RSI, BB, ATR), Sentiment (Twitter volume, Fear&Greed Index, Reddit sentiment via NLP), On-chain (whale flows, exchange inflows, SOPR). Train on 3-5 year history, walk-forward validation. Label: +1 if next-day return > threshold, else -1. Position sizing: Size = (Model Probability - 0.5) × 2 × Max Position × Vol Scaling. Entry: Probability > 0.65. Exit: Probability < 0.35 or -3% hard stop. Sharpe 1.2-1.8 if data quality top-tier. Risk: sentiment alphas fade, overfitting. Source: arXiv 2024-2025, recent papers on sentiment + ML.

14→CointegrationEnhancedCryptoClustering: Identify cointegrated asset clusters via Johansen test. Example: Cluster 1 (payment: BTC, BCH), Cluster 2 (smart contracts: ETH, SOL, AVAX), Cluster 3 (DeFi: LINK, AAVE). Within-cluster pairs: trade mean reversion (spread z-score). Cross-cluster: equal risk allocation. Quarterly re-test cointegration. Quarterly rebalance. Advantage: lower vol from cluster diversification. Risk: clusters break during hard crashes (2022 FTX contagion). Sharpe 1.0-1.3, lower drawdown. Source: Academic papers, QuantConnect.

15→AdvancedBreakoutATRPositionSizing: Detect breakout: price > resistance + 2×ATR(14). Confirmation: range (high-low) >= 2×ATR. Position sizing dynamic on ATR: high ATR (>90th percentile): reduce by 25-50%. Normal: full size. Low ATR: increase 25%. SL: 1.2-1.5×ATR. TP: 1.8-2.5×ATR or 2R multiple. Rebalance daily (ATR recalculation). Works 4H-Daily, all liquid assets. Win rate 40-50%, risk/reward 1:1.5 to 1:3, Sharpe 0.9-1.3. Simple but scales volatility automatically. Source: Mind Math Money, trading systems.
